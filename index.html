A super simple, blazingly fast 2D game "engine"

Why Celenium?

Well... honestly, there's no real reason. I wouldn’t exactly recommend using this "engine" to make levels. Actually, it’s not even an engine—just a level editor. But hey, a man can dream, right? At least you can still make and share levels! You can check out some of them over at the Celenium LVED :)

What is Celenium?

Celenium is a game with a built-in level editor that I created as a stepping stone toward future game development projects. I knew I’d eventually need to build something like this, so I figured I’d tackle it early to avoid the headache of integrating it later in the process.

I plan to open-source Celenium once it reaches a “usable” state—essentially, when users can create some impressive levels (a pretty subjective measure, but I’ll provide more details in the future). Currently, players can create and share their own levels, and if you'd like to publicly share your map, you can upload it to the Celenium LVED .
